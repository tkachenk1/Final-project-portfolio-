Biography

My name is Victoria Tkachenko and I am an undergraduate student at Simon Fraser University majoring in a field of Interactive Arts and Technology. I am considering myself as a starting digital designer specifically focusing on 3-D modeling of the objects and its environment. I find it important to start working with traditional pencil to explore, generate and visually communicate new ideas, then transform the existing design into a digital software. My favorite program for a 3-D modeling is a 3-D computer graphics application called Autodesk Maya where I usually build, texture and light the model. Another important program that I used to produce mathematically precise models like a chair is Rhinoceros 3D.
