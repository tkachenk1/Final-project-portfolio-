This Chair is Not a Chair

The Problem
The aim of this group project is to plan a parametric sitting surface applying rapid sketching for a quick prototyping of ideas, further developed by CAD tools supporting surface and solid modeling.

Limitations
One of the requirements for this project is to consider human dimension so the chair can suit one individual. Our group had to be focused on the proportions of the final model where we were not allowed to exceed the limits of a 24"x24" bounding box. Moreover, the final model of the chair should provide an aesthetically satisfying form such that it can be utilized as an art object.

Solutions
There are three main steps used to create the solution to this problem: sketching, digital modeling and physical modeling.
